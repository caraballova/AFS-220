Laser Lipo

Laser lipolysis is a minimally invasive procedure that uses heat from fiber-optic lasers at various wavelengths to melt body fat. This is a different process than liposuction, which involves removing fat from the body using vaccum suction.

Laser lipolysis can be effective on its own for fat removal. However, intergrating laser lipolysis with traditional liposuction generally produces the best results for tightening and shrinking skin..... This is especially true for individuals who wnat to lose abdomincal fat, but also need the skin to retract.
